## Loom Post-Recording Feature Inventory (verified July 2026)

Loom is owned by Atlassian (acquired 2023); docs now live at support.atlassian.com/loom and the legacy support.loom.com. Plans referenced throughout: **Starter (Free, $0)**, **Business ($18/user/mo)**, **Business + AI ($24/user/mo)**, **Enterprise (contact sales)**, plus Education. 14-day Business+AI trial for all (except Education). [pricing](https://www.loom.com/pricing)

---

### 1. Instant share link (copied on stop)
- On finishing a recording, the Loom video link is **automatically copied to clipboard** so you can paste-to-share immediately. Chrome/browsers can block clipboard access, so Loom has a setting/permission flow to re-enable it. This is the core "record → link is ready" UX. [enable-clipboard](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/enable-video-links-to-copy-into-your-clipboard) [share-recording](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/share-your-recording/)
- Can share at a **specific timestamp**: scrub to a point, select Share → link opens the video at that time. [share-recording]
- All plans.

### 2. Video library / workspace (folders, spaces, search)
- **Workspace = Library + Spaces.** Library holds all videos you created; videos are **private to you by default** until shared.
- **Library folders** for personal organization. [org-library-folders](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/360002216638-Organizing-your-Library-Folders)
- **Spaces** = shared areas where workspace members post/discover videos (e.g. town halls, training, demos); privacy per space is **open or closed**. Folders can live in a Space (visible to all Space members) but a folder lives in **only one Space**, whereas a **video can be shared to multiple Spaces**. "Move" = discoverable in one space; "Share" = discoverable in multiple. [use-spaces](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/7324652635549-How-to-use-spaces) [folder-structure](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/8852786720157-Understanding-Folder-Structure-in-Spaces)
- Library views: **Personal, Shared, Team Library**. Search/filter across library.
- **Space gating:** Free = 1 all-company space + up to 50 members; Business = 1 all-company + 3 additional spaces; Enterprise = unlimited spaces + advanced admin controls. [pricing]

### 3. Watch page (player, speed, chapters, CTA, thumbnails)
- **Playback speed** viewer control with exact steps: **0.8x, 1x, 1.2x, 1.5x, 1.7x, 2x, 2.5x**.
- **Chapters** (auto-generated topic segments with jump links) render as navigation on the watch page; auto-chapters are Business+AI/Enterprise. [ai-features](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/loom-ai-features/)
- **Closed captions** toggle at bottom of player when a transcript exists; viewer can pick a caption **translation language** (client-side text overlay only — audio stays original). [transcription-captions](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/loom-video-transcription-and-closed-captions/)
- **CTA button**: add a link button on the share page (Button Text + URL; URL only, no phone/email). Placement: bottom-left/right, top-left/right, or **display at end of video**; customizable color/text. Gated to **Education, Business, Business+AI, Enterprise** (not Free). [add-cta](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/360002241117-How-to-add-and-edit-a-call-to-action-button)
- **Thumbnails**: default frame; **custom thumbnail** (drag-drop image via Settings → Change thumbnail) on Business/Business+AI/Enterprise/Education. **Animated GIF preview** — first few seconds become an animated cover shown on hover in Library and in embeds. [custom-thumbnail](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/add-a-custom-thumbnail/) [gif-preview](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/how-to-create-an-animated-gif-preview/)

### 4. Editing (trim, stitch, add clips)
All editing is **paid-plans only** (Business+). [edit-video](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/edit-your-loom-video/)
- **Trim (waveform editor)**: drag Trim bar handles, zoom for precision. Limits: **max 500 trims per clip**, **5-hour max video length**. (Business, Business+AI, Enterprise)
- **Split & delete sections**: split into two clips; hold-drag to delete a middle portion.
- **Stitch / Add clips**: combine multiple videos — "Add a clip" from recent Looms, keyword search, or paste URL; reorder with move-left/right. (Business+)
- **Edit by Transcript**: highlight words/sentences in transcript to remove that video segment; undo via clock icon. **Business+AI / Enterprise only.**
- **Overlays**: text, arrows, boxes to highlight elements. **Business+AI / Enterprise only.**
- Free plan editing = **GIF thumbnails only** (no trim/stitch). [pricing]

### 5. Transcription + captions (languages)
- **Transcriptions in 50+ languages**, available on **all plans including Free**. Auto language detection; creator can manually correct the language on the transcript. [multi-language](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/13005225753373-Multi-Language-Transcriptions)
- **Closed captions** on all plans; caption **translation** to viewer's chosen language (AI client-side, text-only). [transcription-captions]

### 6. Loom AI (Business+AI / Enterprise unless noted)
- **Auto Titles** — generated from transcript context. **ALL PLANS incl. Free** (only AI feature free). [ai-features]
- **Auto Summaries** — auto-generated after recording, shown in description box. Business+AI/Ent.
- **Auto Chapters** — topic-based sections + jump links. Business+AI/Ent.
- **Tasks** (auto action items). Business+AI/Ent.
- **AI Workflows** — convert video → text document, message, or **Jira bug report** for direct submission (video-to-doc, video-to-Jira). Business+AI/Ent.
- **Filler Word Removal** — trims "um/ah"/rambles from audio + transcript. Can be applied to videos recorded **before** purchase. Business+AI/Ent.
- **Silence Removal** — trims extended silences. Applies to pre-purchase videos too. Business+AI/Ent.
- **Auto meeting recap emails** + **Auto meeting notes**. Business+AI. [pricing]
- **Variables (BETA)** and **transcript translation**. Business+AI.
- Note: auto summaries/chapters/tasks only apply to videos recorded **after** purchasing Business+AI/Enterprise; filler/silence/workflows can be applied retroactively. [ai-features]

### 7. Privacy controls
Three base levels (all plans): [privacy-settings](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/use-looms-privacy-settings/)
- **Anyone with the link** — no login/account required.
- **Workspace/Company members** — login required; admin sets via Workspace Settings → Security → Default permissions.
- **Only people added** — only emails you added when sharing (email-gated).
- **Password protection** — Business, Business+AI, Enterprise (NOT Free). [pricing]
- **Default privacy settings** for new content — Business+.
- **Public link expiration**, **advanced video privacy controls**, **global admin video view**, **exempted groups for public-link creation**, **disable downloads workspace-wide** — Enterprise (some Business+AI). 
- **Video settings**: disable comments, disable emoji reactions, disable downloads per video. [video-settings](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/360002235857-How-to-edit-your-video-settings-disable-comments-emoji-reactions-downloads-and-more)
- Private videos hide previews by default in embeds/Slack (admin can enable). 

### 8. Viewer analytics
- **Viewer Insights** (ALL plans, incl. Free) — **who watched**: logged-in viewers show by name, others show as **Anonymous**. [see-who-viewed](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/see-who-has-viewed-your-video/)
- **Engagement Insights** (Business, Business+AI, Enterprise) — **completion rate** (avg % watched by tracked sessions) and **CTA conversion rate** (% of tracked viewers who clicked the CTA). Per-individual drill-down: **Video views** (times watched), **Completion rate**, **View Times** (when). [engagement-calc](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/360007980598-How-we-calculate-Engagement-Insights)
- **Insights Hub** — aggregated snapshot across recent videos; **Views over time** (by day/week).
- **Engagement Insights CSV data export** (Business+; downloadable user insights on Enterprise). [data-exports](https://support.loom.com/hc/en-us/articles/360012055658-Engagement-Insights-data-exports)
- Note: no fine-grained per-second engagement **graph** documented by name; engagement is completion% + CTA% + per-viewer view times.

### 9. Engagement (reactions, comments)
- **Emoji reactions** — real-time reactions while watching. All plans.
- **Time-stamped comments** — comments anchored to video timestamps. All plans.
- **Video replies** — reply with a video. 
- Creator can disable comments/reactions per video (see §7). [share] [video-settings]

### 10. Embedding
- Embed video into most sites; **animated GIF preview** (hyperlinked looped clip) as fallback for sites/email that block video (e.g. Apple Mail needs remote content enabled). [embedding](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/understand-embedding-videos-gifs-and-thumbnails/)
- Mechanisms: **Chrome extension** auto-embed (GitHub, Gmail), **EmbedSDK** for third-party apps, **Embedly/iFramely** (Medium, Notion, Trello), manual **iFrame** code. All plans (embed available on Free).

### 11. Downloads
- Download source video (MP4); Free supports basic. **Enterprise admins can disable downloads workspace-wide**; per-video download toggle available. [video-settings] [pricing]

### 12. Integrations
- **Slack** (share/embed, Workflow Builder, Block Kit apps → embed in Jira/Trello/Asana/Linear tickets), **Gmail**, **Jira** + **Jira Service Desk** (record in issue descriptions/comments, AI bug reports), **Confluence**, **GitHub**, **Upwork**, **Notion** (embed), **FigJam** (embed), **GitLab** (embed), **Intercom**, **Zendesk**, **Dropbox**. **Salesforce (Beta)** and **Zoom Import** = Enterprise. [integrate](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/integrate-loom-with-other-tools/) [slack](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/use-looms-slack-integration/) [salesforce](https://support.atlassian.com/loom/docs/set-up-looms-salesforce-integration/) [pricing]

### 13. Team / admin
- **SSO/SAML + SCIM** provisioning — Enterprise only. [pricing]
- **User roles & management**, **activity logs**, **content reports**, **custom data retention policies**, **account transfer & content ownership**, **99.95% uptime SLA**, **EBA compliance**, **downloadable user insights**, dedicated support/account manager, custom onboarding — Enterprise.
- **Admin insights** — Business+AI. **Password videos, default privacy, priority support** — Business. **Remove Loom branding, add logos, custom player colors** — Business+.
- Member caps: Free ≤50 members; Business/Enterprise unlimited.

### Free-plan hard limits (matter for clone parity)
- **≤25 videos/person**, **5-minute max recording**, **720p max**, unlimited screenshots, GIF thumbnails only, no trim/stitch, no password, no SSO. Business removes video count/length caps and adds up to **4K**. [pricing]
